Prasiddha College of Engineering & Technology



The Prasiddha College of Engineering & Technology is Sponsored by Sri Sri Siddi Vinayaka Educational Society Amalapuram, The Engg. College is located in Spacious Buildings in Pollution Free and Pleasant Campus. The College is in close Proximity to Red-Bridge, Amalapuram on NH 214 at Anathavaram. The College is affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada, and approved by AICTE, NEW DELHI.

Library


The college has got a well stacked library which has the carpet area of 25,000 sq.ft. The library has more than 35000 volumes of books and a good number of national and international journals and periodicals are subscribed to enhance the intellectual knowledge of the students and staff. /Our college is a member of online IEEE journals. The library has also signed a MoU with DELNET to have access to very rare collection of books. Well-qualified librarians and assistants are always available to render help to the users of the library. The library is computerized and equipped with a Photocopier for taking copies of any relevant article from the reference section of the library. It has got a state-of-the-art digital library with high speed Internet facility.
